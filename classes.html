Classes

Explore the enriching academic journey at Duaa Public School! We offer a diverse range of classes to ignite your child's curiosity and cultivate their talents.. .

Kindergarden Classes

Kindergarten at Duaa Public School is a playful adventure! Our youngest learners explore the world through hands-on activities, sing-alongs, and stories. We build a strong foundation in language, math, and reading, all while fostering creativity and social skills. It's a joyful journey where your child blossoms and makes lifelong friendships!

Art & Drawing

“Of all God's gifts to the sighted man, color is holiest, the most divine, the most solemn.” In art class, we get to be artists just like grown-ups! We use our hands and imaginations to create colorful pictures. We can draw our favorite animals, silly faces, or even our families. Our teacher shows us fun ways to hold crayons and paintbrushes, and we learn new colors like sunshine yellow and grassy green. Sometimes we even get to mix colors together to make new ones! Art time is a blast because it lets us be creative and have fun while learning new things.

Language & Speaking

Language class is where we learn the magic of words! We sing songs and play games that help us sound out new words and learn how to say them clearly. We practice sharing our stories and ideas with our friends, taking turns and listening carefully. Our teachers help us learn new and exciting words, like the names of all the colorful toys in our classroom or the funny sounds different animals make. The more we practice speaking and listening, the better we become at communicating and making new friends!, we sing, play games, and learn magic words.

Religion & History

In our Religion and History class, we explore stories from the past that teach us about special traditions and amazing people. We learn about different ways people celebrate holidays and show kindness to others. We might even hear stories about heroes and leaders who made a difference. This class helps us understand where we come from and how our world became so interesting! It's like taking a fun trip through time to learn cool things – all while making new friends in class! (Note: This avoids mentioning specific religions and keeps it more general for a diverse classroom)

Primary Classes

Primary classes at Duaa Public School are a whirlwind of discovery! Our young learners embark on an exciting journey filled with exploration, laughter, and a strong foundation in core subjects like reading, writing, and math. Through engaging activities, songs, and stories, our dedicated teachers spark a love of learning that will stay with your child for years to come..

Islamic Studies

In Islamic Studies, we embark on a fascinating journey to learn about Islam! We explore stories of Prophets like Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and discover the values they taught us. We practice good manners, like saying "ٱلسَّلَامُ عَلَيْكُمْ","Asalam o Alikum" (peace be upon you) and sharing with others. We also learn basic Arabic words and prayers, like "ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّٰهِ","Alhamdulillah" (praise be to God) for all the good things in our lives. Islamic Studies helps us understand our faith, become better Muslims, become better humans and contribute positively to our community!

Math & Science

Get ready to explore the amazing worlds of math and science in primary class! In math, we'll have adventures with numbers, solving fun puzzles and learning how to add, subtract, and count like whizzes! We'll even play games with shapes and patterns, building cool things all on our own. In science, we'll become like detectives, investigating the world around us. We'll observe plants and animals, discover the secrets of magnets and weather, and learn exciting things about the human body! It's a hands-on journey filled with wonder and discovery!

History & Geography

In History and Geography class, we travel through time and explore amazing places! We learn about incredible people from the past and how they shaped our world. We might even build mini pyramids from Egypt or create a timeline of important historical events. Geography takes us on exciting adventures around the globe! We discover different countries, learn about their cultures, and explore the fascinating features of our planet, from mountains and oceans to deserts and rain-forests. It's like taking a trip around the world without ever leaving the classroom!

High School Classes

High school at Duaa Public School is all about exploration and preparation. Students delve deeper into core subjects, tackling increasingly complex concepts. They can also discover their passions through a diverse range of electives, from science labs and foreign languages to art studios and performing arts. Our dedicated teachers guide students as they prepare for higher education and future careers, fostering critical thinking, effective communication, and a thirst for lifelong learning.

Mathematics & Critical thinking

Mathematics & Critical Thinking: Unlocking the Power of Problem-Solving

High school math at Duaa Public School is more than just calculations and equations. It's a springboard for developing critical thinking skills that will benefit you far beyond the classroom. We'll move beyond rote memorization and delve into the "why" behind mathematical concepts. You'll learn to analyze problems from various angles, identify patterns, and formulate logical arguments to reach your solutions. Through collaborative projects and real-world applications, you'll hone your critical thinking skills, learn to break down complex problems into manageable steps, and develop strategies for innovative solutions. This focus on critical thinking will not only equip you to excel in advanced math courses but also prepare you for success in any academic or professional pursuit.


Math & Critical Thinking: The Language of Logic and Reasoning

Math in high school at Duaa Public School becomes a powerful tool for logical reasoning and clear communication. Imagine math as a language – a language of precise symbols and structures used to express ideas and solve problems. We'll explore the connections between mathematical concepts and the underlying logic that governs them. You'll learn to dissect arguments, identify logical fallacies, and construct clear and concise proofs. Our engaging curriculum challenges you to think critically, analyze information objectively, and develop a rigorous approach to problem-solving. These skills will not only make you a stronger mathematician but also equip you to become a more confident and articulate communicator in all walks of life.


Computer Science & Biology

Computer Science - Unveiling the Digital World

In our high school computer science program, you'll delve into the fascinating world behind your favorite gadgets and online platforms. We'll explore the fundamental principles of coding, learning how to create programs that solve problems and bring ideas to life. You'll experiment with different programming languages, build interactive applications, and gain insights into cybersecurity and data analysis. As you unlock the secrets of the digital world, you'll develop critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and a foundation for future careers in the ever-evolving tech industry.









Biology - Exploring the Wonders of Life

High school biology at Duaa Public School is an exciting journey into the intricate world of living things. We'll dissect cells, explore the building blocks of life, and unravel the mysteries of genetics. You'll delve into the fascinating processes of human anatomy and physiology, understanding how your own body works. Our interactive labs provide a platform for hands-on learning, where you'll conduct experiments, analyze data, and develop critical scientific thinking skills. This program will spark your curiosity about the natural world, prepare you for further studies in life sciences, and equip you with a deeper appreciation for the wonders of life.






Physics & Chemistry

Physics: Unveiling the Universe's Secrets

Physics at Duaa Public School is more than just formulas and textbook problems. It's your chance to become a detective, unraveling the mysteries that govern the universe. We'll explore the fundamental laws of motion, delve into the fascinating world of forces and energy, and unlock the secrets of electricity and magnetism. Through interactive labs and thought-provoking experiments, you'll gain hands-on experience with scientific tools and develop critical thinking skills as you analyze data and draw conclusions. This program isn't just about memorizing facts; it's about understanding the "why" behind the natural world. By exploring the universe's wonders, physics ignites your curiosity, fosters a problem-solving mindset, and prepares you for further studies in engineering, technology, or any field that requires a deep understanding of the physical world.

Chemistry: The Magic of Matter and Change

High school chemistry at Duaa Public School is an exciting journey into the world of atoms and molecules. We'll witness the magic of chemical reactions firsthand, explore the periodic table in all its glory, and unlock the secrets of chemical bonding. Our engaging curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning through interactive labs. You'll perform experiments, analyze reactions, and develop critical thinking skills as you explore cause-and-effect relationships. This program goes beyond memorizing formulas; it's about understanding the "how" and "why" of chemical change. Chemistry fosters your scientific curiosity, sharpens your problem-solving skills, and equips you with a foundation for future studies in medicine, pharmacy, environmental science, and various other exciting fields.
